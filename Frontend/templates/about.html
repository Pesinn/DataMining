The application has access to over 12.5 million news articles, written in English, published by 89 news sources. Each of these articles have been visible on a landing page of their source. It is possible to search for any news topic in the whole dataset.

10 most frequent named entities can be found for any topic. It can also be displayed as word cloud

Sentiment analysis can be displayed for any news articles topic. Also, sentiment analysis can be compared between many different topics. To compare sentiment analysis of e.g. covid and pfizer, it must be divided by | character. Example: covid | pfizer
